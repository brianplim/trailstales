Essential Travel Tips

  • Pack light: Bring versatile clothing and only essentials to avoid heavy luggage.
  • Stay hydrated: Always carry a refillable water bottle to keep refreshed.
  • Backup documents: Keep digital and physical copies of your passport, visa, and itinerary.
  • Local customs: Respect cultural norms and dress codes.
  • Learn basic phrases: Knowing key greetings and phrases helps with communication.

Money Saving Hacks

  • Use local transportation and avoid taxis when possible.
  • Eat where the locals eat for authentic and affordable food.
  • Book accommodations in advance to get better rates.
