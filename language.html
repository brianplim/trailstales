Language

Learning a few key phrases in the local language can enrich your travel experience and help you connect with people more meaningfully.

Essential Travel Phrases

  • Hello: Hola (Spanish), Bonjour (French), Namaste (Hindi)
  • Thank you: Gracias (Spanish), Merci (French), Dhanyavaad (Hindi)
  • Where is…?: Useful for asking directions, e.g., ¿Dónde está…? (Spanish)
  • Help: Important for emergencies, e.g., Au secours (French), Madad (Hindi)

Language Learning Apps

  • Duolingo – Fun, free app for beginners.
  • Memrise – Focuses on vocabulary and real-life conversations.
  • Busuu – Offers interactive lessons and practice with native speakers.
