Health

Maintaining your health on the trail is crucial for a successful adventure. Proper preparation and awareness can help you avoid common travel illnesses and injuries.

Tips for Staying Healthy While Traveling

  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water and use water purification methods when necessary.
  • Vaccinations: Ensure your vaccinations are up to date based on your destination. See CDC Travel Health for more info.
  • First Aid Kit: Pack a travel-friendly first aid kit including bandages, antiseptic wipes, pain relievers, and any personal medications.
  • Food Safety: Eat freshly cooked foods and avoid street food that may be unsafe.
