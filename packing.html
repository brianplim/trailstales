Packing

Packing smart is key to a smooth travel experience. Whether you’re heading for a weekend getaway or a multi-week adventure, knowing what to bring can make all the difference.

Essential Packing Tips

  • Make a checklist: Write down what you need based on your destination, weather, and activities planned.
  • Pack versatile clothing: Choose items that can be layered and worn in multiple ways.
  • Limit shoes: Shoes take up the most space—try to bring only what’s necessary.
  • Use packing cubes: Keep your suitcase organized and save space.
  • Don’t forget chargers and adapters: Essential for keeping devices powered abroad.

Travel Gear Must-Haves

  • Lightweight backpack or suitcase
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Travel-sized toiletries
  • First aid kit
  • Travel pillow and eye mask
